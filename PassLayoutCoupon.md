# Layout of a Coupon Pass #

This pass style is appropriate for coupons, vouchers, special offers and other discounts.

## Supported Images ##

  * Icon
  * Logo
  * Strip Image

## Field constraints ##

Up to 4 secondary and auxiliary fields
